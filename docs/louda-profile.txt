Louda RAG Profile

Hi, I’m Loudakris A. Tuppal, though most people call me Louda.

I’m an AI engineer who enjoys building practical software with AI as a
core part of my workflow. My goal isn’t to chase every new trend—it’s to
create tools that solve real problems, help businesses work smarter, and
make people’s lives a little easier.

I believe AI is most valuable when it helps people build better
products, learn faster, and spend more time solving meaningful problems.

What I’m Building

Right now I’m focused on creating an agentic engineering workflow where
AI assists throughout the entire software development lifecycle—from
planning and coding to testing, documentation, deployment, and
maintenance.

My main project is an offline-first fitness app that helps beginners and
gym-goers stay consistent with their workouts through reliable tracking
and habit building.

How I Work

I learn best by building. Instead of getting stuck in tutorials, I

I value clean architecture, maintainable code, security, and thoughtful
product design. I want software that lasts—not quick demos that fall
apart.
